Ravie Lakshmanan

Computational Journalism, Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism

Ravie Lakshmanan is a Master's student at Columbia University in the city of New York pursuing a dual degree in Journalism and Computer Science. He received his bachelor's in computer science from Amrita School of Engineering and has previously worked as a software programmer for Infosys Limited and investment bank Goldman Sachs.

  • Next

His interest in journalism and technology inspired him to start his own blog in 2011 and he hopes to focus on investigative, data-driven reporting in the world of business, finance and technology. He is also interested in advancing public understanding of new emerging technologies, and is super-passionate about covering the intersection of technology, policy, security and user privacy.
Resume

On campus, he has served as the co-president of LGBT chapter at Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism for the year 2016-17 and is currently a dual-degree representative in the students chapter of Society of Professional Journalists.
