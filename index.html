Ravie Lakshmanan is a journalist whose work is geared towards cybersecurity and open-source intelligence. He is a master's graduate with a dual degree in Journalism and Computer Science from Columbia University, New York. He received his bachelor's in computer science from Amrita School of Engineering, and has previously worked as a software programmer for Infosys Limited and investment bank Goldman Sachs.

Following graduation, Ravie served as a data journalist and reporter at Financial Planning covering FinTech, cryptocurrencies and wealth management, and has worked as a software engineer developing news products for Scroll.in. He is currently a senior OSINT analyst and dis/mis-information researcher at Logically, and covers all things cybersecurity and privacy at The Hacker News.

Ravie's current areas of interest and exploration are new emerging technologies, algorithmic accountability and fairness in intelligent systems, and the broader intersection of technology, policy, security and user privacy.
